Version: 0.1.0
Date: 2026-06-15
  Features:
    - Adds a "Teleport to buddy" hotkey (default Ctrl+T) that jumps you to a buddy's view location.
    - Press the hotkey again to return to exactly where you were standing before the jump.
    - Teleports to where the buddy is looking (remote view / camera), not their physical character.
    - Top-left toolbar button opens a draggable panel with a dropdown to choose which buddy to follow.
    - Finds a safe non-colliding spot near the target and crosses surfaces (e.g. onto other planets).
